# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to building a welcoming, safe, and respectful community for everyone.
Contributors, maintainers, and participants are expected to collaborate in good faith,
value different perspectives, and treat each other with professionalism and empathy.

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including issues, pull requests,
discussions, documentation, chats, events, and any other community interaction linked
to this project.

## Expected Behavior

Community members are expected to:

- Be respectful in language and tone, even during disagreement.
- Assume positive intent and ask clarifying questions before escalating.
- Give constructive feedback focused on ideas, code, and outcomes.
- Be inclusive and considerate of people with different backgrounds and experiences.
- Respect boundaries, privacy, and consent.
- Help maintain a collaborative environment where others can safely participate.

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not acceptable in any project space:

- Harassment, intimidation, threats, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory or demeaning comments based on identity or personal attributes.
- Sexualized language, unwanted sexual attention, or inappropriate physical contact.
- Trolling, baiting, or deliberately disruptive behavior.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Repeatedly derailing discussions away from productive collaboration.
- Any conduct that would reasonably make participation unsafe or hostile.

## Reporting Concerns

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, report it to
the maintainers as soon as possible.

Please include:

- What happened and where it happened.
- Any relevant links, screenshots, or logs.
- Names or handles of people involved and possible witnesses.

Maintainers will review reports promptly and handle them as confidentially as possible.
No one will be retaliated against for making a good-faith report.

## Enforcement

Maintainers are responsible for interpreting and enforcing this Code of Conduct. They
may take any action they deem appropriate in response to violations, including:

- Warning the person involved.
- Removing or editing content.
- Temporarily restricting participation.
- Permanently removing access to project spaces.

Enforcement decisions are made to protect the community and preserve healthy
collaboration.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project-managed spaces and in public spaces
when an individual is representing the project or its community.

## Acknowledgment

By participating in this project, you agree to follow this Code of Conduct.

Thank you for helping make this community respectful, inclusive, and productive.
